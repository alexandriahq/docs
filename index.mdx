Ambient is a local-first continual learning system that runs quietly in the background on your desktop. It observes your work — the documents you write, the tasks you juggle, the goals you pursue — and builds a structured, encrypted memory that stays entirely on your machine. When you need an AI agent to help you, Ambient hands it rich, accurate context instead of asking you to explain yourself from scratch.

## What Ambient Does

Ambient runs as a background process on your desktop. Throughout your day it captures signals from your OS — active windows, documents, browser activity — and distills them into a structured memory that grows smarter over time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the desktop app">
    Download and install Ambient for macOS. The app runs as a menu-bar icon and starts capturing context automatically once you sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to your account">
    Authenticate with your Ambient account. All your settings and preferences are tied to your account while your actual memory stays local.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ambient learns your work">
    As you work, Ambient builds observations, tracks your evolving intents, and mines long-running tasks — all stored in an encrypted local database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Give agents real context">
    When you invoke an AI agent, Ambient prepares a handoff summary so the agent knows exactly what you've been working on — no re-explaining needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Local-First Storage" icon="hard-drive">
    All memory is stored in an encrypted database on your machine. Nothing leaves your device without your explicit permission.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Redaction" icon="eye-slash">
    Ambient scrubs passwords, API keys, and personal data before anything is written to memory.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intent Tracking" icon="bullseye">
    Beyond recording what you did, Ambient infers what you're trying to accomplish and tracks how your goals evolve.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Task Mining" icon="list-check">
    Long-horizon tasks that span multiple sessions are automatically discovered and surfaced.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agent Handoffs" icon="share-nodes">
    Structured context summaries give AI agents everything they need to pick up exactly where you left off.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Privacy Controls" icon="sliders">
    Block specific apps, domains, or time windows from capture at any time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
