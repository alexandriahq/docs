Once setup is complete, Ambient runs by default.

You do not need to start a session or write notes manually. Keep working and Ambient will build memory from the activity you allowed it to capture.

## Daily workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Work normally">
    Use your editor, browser, terminal, meetings, and docs as usual.

    Ambient observes the sources you enabled and turns them into useful context.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use the overlay when you want visibility">
    Click the pop-out icon in Ambient to open the live overlay.

    The overlay helps you confirm what Ambient is seeing without interrupting your work.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review memory occasionally">
    Open **Memory** to see what Ambient has learned.

    Use it to check that important work is represented and private work is excluded.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Respond to handoff notifications">
    When Ambient finds work an agent can take on, it sends a handoff notification.

    Review the proposed task, then accept it, adjust it, or ignore it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Ambient only learns from capture sources and integrations you enable. You can update capture settings at any time.
</Note>
