Ambient is currently invite-only. After you receive access, install the desktop app and complete the setup flow.

<Note>
  Look for an invite email from Alexandria or `access@workos.dev`. If you do not see it, check your spam folder.
</Note>

## Install

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Open your Ambient invite and create your account.

    Ambient uses WorkOS for sign-in, so the access email may come from `access@workos.dev`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download Ambient">
    Download both installers for your operating system. Install **Ambient Bridge** first, then **Ambient App**.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="macOS: Ambient Bridge" icon="download" href="https://api.alexandria.so/downloads/apps/ambient-bridge/alpha/darwin/arm64/latest.dmg">
        Step 1 of 2. Download the Apple Silicon DMG.
      </Card>

      <Card title="macOS: Ambient App" icon="download" href="https://api.alexandria.so/downloads/apps/ambient-app/alpha/darwin/arm64/latest.dmg">
        Step 2 of 2. Download the Apple Silicon DMG.
      </Card>

      <Card title="Windows: Ambient Bridge" icon="download" href="https://api.alexandria.so/downloads/apps/ambient-bridge/alpha/win32/x64/latest.exe">
        Step 1 of 2. Download the Windows x64 EXE.
      </Card>

      <Card title="Windows: Ambient App" icon="download" href="https://api.alexandria.so/downloads/apps/ambient-app/alpha/win32/x64/latest.exe">
        Step 2 of 2. Download the Windows x64 EXE.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install Bridge">
    Open the Ambient Bridge installer and launch Bridge.

    Bridge handles sign-in and agent connections for the desktop app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install Ambient App">
    Open the Ambient App installer and launch Ambient.

    On macOS, move Ambient to **Applications** if prompted. On Windows, follow the installer prompts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with the account from your invite.

    Keep Bridge running after sign-in. Ambient uses it to connect securely to your account and integrations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give permissions">
    Grant the permissions Ambient asks for during setup.

    These may include screen recording, accessibility, microphone, input monitoring, and other system permissions depending on what you enable.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure capture">
    Choose what Ambient can capture.

    You can allow the sources you want and exclude private apps, domains, or workflows before Ambient starts learning from your activity.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Codex or Claude Code">
    Connect your coding agent from the Ambient setup flow or from **Settings** > **Integrations**.

    Once connected, Ambient can prepare context and handoffs for that agent.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start working">
    You're done.

    Ambient runs in the background by default. Keep working as usual and let Ambient build memory from your active work.
  </Step>
</Steps>
